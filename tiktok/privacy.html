Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2024

  • Device Information: Device type, screen size (for game compatibility)

The information we collect is only used for:

Your game data is saved through TikTok's official cloud storage service. We:

4. Information Sharing

We promise:

  • The game includes anti-addiction reminder mechanisms

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Stop using the game service at any time
  • Request deletion of your game data
  • Understand how we use your information

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us:

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. For major changes, we will notify you within the game. Continued use of the game indicates your agreement to the updated policy.
